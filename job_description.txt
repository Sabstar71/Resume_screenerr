Job Title: Data Scientist

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning. You will work closely with stakeholders to extract insights and build predictive models that drive business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets using Python and SQL.
- Build predictive models using scikit-learn, XGBoost, or other ML libraries.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business opportunities.
- Create dashboards and visualizations using Power BI, Tableau, or matplotlib/seaborn.
- Clean, transform, and preprocess raw data for analysis and modeling.

Requirements:
- Strong proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, seaborn).
- Experience with machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or PyTorch).
- Good knowledge of SQL and relational databases.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) is a plus.
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
- Bachelor’s or Master’s in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or related field.

Nice to have:
- Experience with NLP, time-series analysis, or deep learning.
- Working knowledge of version control (Git).
- Participation in Kaggle competitions or open-source projects.
